Once hotels trust the product, it will more likely make them to use Traveloka compared to other OTAs\n- **Optimization:** It creates a mutual benefit between us and the hotel, where when the hotel is able to optimize their revenue, it will also increase our commission revenue.\n\n\n\n# Objective\n\nThe primary objective of this project is to build a scalable promotion tiering.
